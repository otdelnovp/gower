predict your audience

Gower Street Analytics is an ambitious project to measurably improve the efficiency of the global theatrical market through advanced data science. We're partnering with exhibitors, distributors, and other key players to help films reach a bigger audience.

What do we do?

We help theatrical distribution and exhibition make data-driven dating and booking decisions.

Our bespoke systems are powered by a global theatrical forecast engine, created by our team of data scientists and monitored by our team of film analysts.

Distributors will benefit from a state-of-the-art Distribution Strategy system, while Exhibitors will be able to take advantage of our Booking Optimisation tool.

Who are we anyway?

Hollywood meets Research

We combine our Hollywood industry experience with cutting-edge technology: functional programming, topological data analysis, and machine learning. The result is faster, more accurate information for better decisions in theatrical distribution and exhibition.

Our tech stack includes Racket, Ocaml, Haskell, Apache Storm, Mahoot, Spark, Lucene/Solr, and PostgreSQL.
